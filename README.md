# ProNest Nigeria

> Nigeria's AI-powered digital ecosystem where people learn, earn, connect and grow.

## Vision

To build Africa's most impactful digital ecosystem that empowers individuals, businesses, schools, governments, farmers, creators and communities through technology.

## Mission

ProNest exists to help people:

- Learn new skills
- Find opportunities
- Grow businesses
- Connect with trusted people
- Access digital services
- Improve their communities
- Reach global markets

## Core Principles

- People First
- Trust & Transparency
- Innovation
- Accessibility
- Community Growth
- Economic Empowerment
